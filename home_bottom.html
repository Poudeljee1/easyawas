ABOUT EASYAWAS

Nepal's Trusted Rental Platform

EasyAwas was founded with one mission — to make renting in Nepal simple, transparent, and fair for both landlords and tenants. We eliminate brokers, hidden fees, and fake listings.

From Kathmandu to Pokhara, from Biratnagar to Butwal — we connect real people with real homes across all 77 districts of Nepal.

  • Zero broker fees — direct landlord to tenant
  • Verified listings with real photos
  • Secure online payments in NPR
  • 24/7 customer support in Nepali & English
  • Trusted by 18,000+ renters across Nepal
